Jiashuo Fan

Welcome! My name is Jiashuo Fan. I am currently a PhD student at Duke University (advisor: Prof. Lawrence Carin). Previously I completed my M.S. at Tsinghua University (advisor: Prof. Shaolun Huang) and have been mentored by Dr. Lei Zhang (IDEA / Microsoft) on collaborative projects.

My research interests are in in-context learning, diffusion / generative models, and semi-supervised learning, with applications in vision-language learning and spatial biological data reconstruction.

I'm open to collaboration and discussions. Let's connect and explore possibilities together!
